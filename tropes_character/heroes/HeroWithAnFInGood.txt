Like the minion with an f in evil, the Hero With an F in Good most of the time is basically a nice person deep down, but while the Minion with an F in Evil became a punch clock villain because evil is cool, but his pet the dog personality foils his bosses' plans, this character sometimes daydreams of becoming one of the hero's true companions, or being the hero by himself, but serious character flaws make it a poor career goal. He doesn't have a problem with doing heroic things, but he does have trouble when it comes to other aspects of being good.

An occurrence in most forms of media, where a character who is usually a cloud cuckoolander or a dojikko, will very often talk with his shadow like it's a real person.

Compare: imaginary friend, consulting mister puppet, not-so-imaginary friend, and mirror monologue.

Contrast: living shadow, in which the shadow really is a living being.

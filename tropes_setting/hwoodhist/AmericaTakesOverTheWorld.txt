The USA is capable not only of exterminating with its long-range nuclear arsenal, but also using ground forces to successfully invade and occupy any country on Earth including China and India. Given that the USA chews through about half the entire world's military spending on its own, it might still have this kind of power in forty years or so.

Thus, a common conceit of sci-fi and some satire is for The USA to take over the world. Jokes about entire countries becoming the n+50th state are common. The recent economic shift in the new millenium will put this theory to the test.

sub-trope of both take over the world and expanded states of america. Compare united space of america. See also japan takes over the world, and china takes over the world.

Opposing tropes are divided states of america, fallen states of america, and invaded states of america.
